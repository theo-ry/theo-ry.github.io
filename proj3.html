App Design

Planted

Prototype of an app that aims to help encourage the community to bond through community gardening.

MORE
The Challenge

To come up with a mobile application that promotes health, fitness and interaction among the community of a HDB estate. and designing an interactive prototype for it within a few weeks. The design was made for the iPhone 8 Plus.

The Process

I started off with doing research on the current activities that residents like to do in community. The idea was then developed, more research was carried out and surveys were sent out to get a rough idea of how the idea would do. After getting satisfactory results, more research was done, wireframes were sketched, a style guide was made, mockups were made and linked to create the interactive prototype. After user testing and reiterations, the final product was born.
