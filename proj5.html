VR Development

Risk

An interactive installation on the risks of climate change. VR and desktop versions are available.

MORE
The Challenge

To design an interactive exhibition in line the theme "Risk" within a 3 months. The main challenge was creating this exhibition with no knowledge about VR development and extremely basic CS knowledge during the pandemic.

The Process

I started with researching on current interactive exhibitions and took note of the ones that caught my attention. I also took note of their strengths and weaknesses. Ideation came next and after many sketches and reiterations, the idea was finalised. Next came research, coding, bug fixing, more research and bug fixing. After 3 months of struggling, the exhibition was completed.

Client

Originally ArtScience Museum Singapore but this was cancelled due to the pandemic.
